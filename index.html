About Me

My name is Dylan Thomas, I am 26 years old and from Salt Lake City, Utah. I started teaching myself how to code shortly after high school and enjoyed it so much I enrolled in the coding bootcamp through the University of Utah in 2019. I am the youngest child of three boys. I have a 7 year old white labrador name Casper who still acts like a puppy.

Outside of coding some of my hobbies include snowboarding in the winter season, golfing and playing guitar. I have played guitar for more than 15 years and enjoy playing and creating different types music with others. If I'm not in class you will most likely find me on the side of a mountain this winter catching up on all the snowboarding I missed last year due to injury.

I look forward to starting this course and growing my coding abilities both personally and professionally!
